What is our brand about?

Miss K Beauty is a widely recognised skincare beauty brand that started on the 24 March 2012. It's main office is based in London, United Kingdom and has over 25 sub-offices all of Europe and will be branching in to Africa as of 2018. Miss K beauty was started by Khrestie Kanku-Kapuya, the CEO. This wonderful beauty brand not only sells Natural and organic skincare products that accomodates people of of all skin types but it is dermatologically approved. The skincare prodcuts are made with avocado oil extracts, aloe vera gel and much more safe, earth friendly and pet friendly ingredients. Miss k beauty has also started a female empowerment program where they recruit women from the ages of 16-25. The aim of this program isto form young women with skills such as perserverance, resilience, confidence and this program will help them grow mental and emotionally. The purpose of Miss K beauty is to help women gain confidence not only through their skincare but also through their everyday life by using our beauty skincaer products.
